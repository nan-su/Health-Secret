Leg Exercises


Stand with feet hip-width apart before stepping your right foot back, and lowering into lunge stance with the left knee over the ankle.

Bring your arms over your head and hinge forward from the waist.

Lower the chest forward toward the thighs as your arms reach forward.

Lift the right leg while straightening the left.

Hold for 3 breaths before returning to the starting lunge position.

Do 3 reps; switch legs and repeat.
