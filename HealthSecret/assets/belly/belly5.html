Move 5 (Intermediate: Discus Throw)


Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, arms extended out to sides at shoulder level.

Lunge to right with right leg, rotating torso to right.

Quickly shift weight to left leg, bend knee, and push off left foot to hop up as you turn to left and swing right arm across body (ad if throwing a discus).

Do 10 reps. Switch sides; repeat.
