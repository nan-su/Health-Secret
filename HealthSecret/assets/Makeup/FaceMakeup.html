Face makeup tips



1. Before you begin the make up, wash your face to be clear and smooth.

2. Apply a light moisturizer that is suitable for your skin type.

3. Apply foundation on the skin surface to make your make up last longer.

4. Apply a sheer foundation that matches your skin tone.

5. Use a make up sponge while applying the foundation.

6. Apply concealer to any red, dark circles or discolored spots in your face.

7. Use a make up brush and apply powder all over your face.

8. Apply a small amount of blush to the apples of your cheeks.

9. Once everything is done, take a fluffy powder brush and blend everything together so that there are no visible lines or powders.
