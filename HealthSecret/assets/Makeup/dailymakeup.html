Daily makeup tips



1. Before applying any make up, wipe off your face thoroughly.

2. Apply a moisturizer after you clean your face.

3. Use concealer that matches your skin tone and apply to cover any spots on your face.

4. Use a liquid or powder foundation that works better for you.

5. Use a brush to apply blush on your cheekbones.

6. Use an eye primer or a little foundation with powder to apply on your eyelid before applying eye shadow.

7. Apply an eye shadow of your choice

8. Line your upper eyes with liquid or pencil eyeliner.

9. Curl your lashes and apply a coat of black or brown mascara.

Makeup tips for a natural look

1. Wash your face with soap and water, and clean your face with a tissue before applying make up.

2. Use a moisturizer and apply gently on your face.

3. Apply a natural light foundation on your face.

4. Use a concealer to cover the dark or red spots on your face.

5. Use eye shadow that matches your skin tone and apply on the upper part of your eyelid.

6. Line your upper eyelid with liquid eyeliner.

7. Curl your lashes with eyelash curler and apply a coat of mascara.

8. Apply blush on your cheekbones.

9. Use a neutral lip color that matches your natural lip color.
