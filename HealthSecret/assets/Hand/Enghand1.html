Make a Fist Stretch


Hand and finger exercises can help strengthen your hands and fingers, increase your range of motion, and give you pain relief.

Stretch only until you feel tightness.

You shouldn't feel pain.

Start with this simple stretch: • Make a gentle fist, wrapping your thumb across your fingers.

• Hold for 30 to 60 seconds. Release and spread your fingers wide.

• Repeat with both hands at least four times.
